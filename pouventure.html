About The Game

Pouventure is a turn based combat game where you play as a girl named Avery. The goal of the game is to fight monsters in a random generated dungeon and get to the end to save Pou.
It features 3 enemies with all different kinds of attacks, a boss fight at the end and a random generated dungeon system.

My experience

This is my first time I made a turn based combat game. I got to learn how to keep track of the player stats and enemy stats. I have also created a random dungeon generator in this project.

My Contributions

I made the player movement, dungeon generation, battle system and the enemy behaviour.
