About This Game

Honey Towers is a tower defense game where you defend your hive from wasps and ants. You play as a bee holding a box and you can fly around placing your towers. You can collect honey around the map to buy towers at the shop.

My experience

I enjoyed working on this project as I learned how to make a tower defense system.

My Contributions

I contributed to all the programming of this project.
