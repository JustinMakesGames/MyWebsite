About This Game

Wizcape is a game where you play in a randomly generated dungeon. You fight monsters with weapons you find along the way. After all the rooms, there is a boss fight at the end.

My experience

Wizcape is a game where I learned to use a proper real time weapon system.

My Contributions

My contributions to the project are the movement, the pick up system, the weapon system and the inventory system.
